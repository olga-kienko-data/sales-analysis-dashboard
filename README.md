#  Sales Analysis & ABC Classification Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes global sales performance of a company operating in both online and offline channels.

The analysis includes:

* Data cleaning and preparation
* Sales analysis by categories, countries and regions
* Online vs Offline comparison
* Shipping time analysis
* Trend analysis (yearly & monthly)
* ABC analysis using Pareto principle

---

##  Key Metrics

* Total Orders: 1330
* Total Revenue: 1,702,129,408
* Total Profit: 501,434,459
* Countries Covered: 46
* Overall Margin: 29.46%

---

##  Key Insights

###  Category Performance

* High-volume categories: Household, Office Supplies, Vegetables, Baby Food
* High-profit categories: Office Supplies, Snacks, Clothes, Cosmetics (30–40% margin)
* Low-margin categories: Fruits, Vegetables, Meat

 High volume does not always mean high profitability → margin optimization required

---

###  Geographic Analysis

* Europe is the main revenue and profit driver
* Eastern Europe is the strongest sub-region
* Key markets: Czech Republic, Ukraine, Poland, Romania

 Business is highly concentrated in European markets

---

###  Sales Channels

* Offline → higher total revenue
* Online → higher margin

 Online channel shows strong growth potential

---

###  Shipping Analysis

* Data cleaned (removed invalid delivery intervals)
* No strong correlation between shipping time and profit

 Profit depends more on product category and pricing than delivery speed

---

###  Trends & Seasonality

* Growth from 2010 to peak in 2013–2014
* Slight decline after 2016
* Strong seasonality: peak sales in April–September

---

###  Weekly Performance

* Peak: Saturday
* Strong: Sunday, Monday
* Weakest: Thursday

 Best time for promotions → weekend

---

###  ABC Analysis

* Small number of categories generate >80% of revenue and profit
* Category A includes:

  * Office Supplies
  * Beverages
  * Cereal
  * Personal Care
  * Snacks

 Business heavily relies on key product groups (Pareto principle)

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin categories (Clothes, Cosmetics, Snacks, Beverages)
* Optimize or restructure low-margin categories (Fruits, Meat, Vegetables)
* Expand online channel (higher profitability)
* Strengthen presence in key European markets
* Concentrate marketing on high-performing days (Sat–Mon)
* Improve data quality (validate delivery dates, missing regions)

##  Conclusion

The analysis shows that business performance is driven by a limited number of high-performing product categories and regions.
Focusing on these areas, along with margin optimization and channel development, can significantly improve overall profitability.
